about

solving the problem of informal marketplaces

current informal marketplaces for designer gear take place in localized buying and selling groups.
however, issues such as knockoffs, unfair pricing, and security plague this industry.
hypebox solves these issues by centralizing the local designer buying and selling scene through our locker box.
users can sell their products or purchase the ones listed in each box. we gaurantee authenticity and ensure fair pricing.


how does it work?

users create an account using hypebox and download our app, then find nearby boxes to visit.
to see how each process works, visit the buying or selling tab.
